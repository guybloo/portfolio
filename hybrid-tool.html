Hybrid Tool

A tool for tying palm tree branches

My parents are date farmers and our fruits are super juicy

Having such big fruits causes heavy branches to break

To prevent it, we are tying the branches together to gain more strength

We can cut one stick from the branch with the knife in the bottom of the tool

When it is open, we can close it on the branches until they are coupled

The stick can be pushed through the hole in the bottom of the loop and around the branches until we can tie it

This tool acts as an extension to the arm, allowing greater reach to higher branches

It is ecological, using sticks instead of plastic, saving approximately 800KG of waste per year!
