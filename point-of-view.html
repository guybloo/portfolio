Point of View

Hidden patterns in a points cloud for Tel Aviv Museum of Arts

Chose some patterns

Generated a cloud with a parametric algorithm

The cloud made of noise, patterns and intersecting part

Optimized parameters and resolution

Merged several clouds and got their bounding shape

Placed the result in a 3D model of Tel Aviv Museum of Arts

Rendered the specific points of view to simulate the experience

This project was ordered by the museum from Amit Zoran's Hybrid Lab in the Hebrew University

It was meant to be placed in the museum's main staircase
